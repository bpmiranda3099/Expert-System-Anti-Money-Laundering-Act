About the AMA Expert System

This Anti-Money Laundering Act (AMLA) Expert System is a conceptual project developed for academic and compliance-related research purposes. It is not an official tool or service.

The system is designed to simulate approach to AMLA compliance under the Philippine Anti-Money Laundering Act, providing illustrative guidance and example-based insights only.

Please note that this is not intended for actual regulatory use, and any recommendations or outputs should not be considered professional or legal advice.
